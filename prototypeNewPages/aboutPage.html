What is this Program?

The current program we are developing is a web-based interactive story. This type of game is very similar to a "choose your own adventure" game. In this game, you must make difficult choices that establish the protagonist's personality and actions. Be careful as these decisions can have profound and unexpected results and impacts.



What is the story?

You play as an archeologist that has been tasked to investigate a desert with particularly unusual traits. During your exploration you find yourself in a new world.
